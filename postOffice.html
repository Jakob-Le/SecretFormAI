If you are under the age of 16 or your passport is older than 15 years, you must apply for a new passport.

Passport Renewal

Online

Complete application

Required:

Most recent passport

Travel plans

Digital photo

By Mail

Complete form DS-82

Mail the completed DS-82, passport photo, payment, and most recent passport. The address to send to is on the application form.

New Passport

Complete form DS-11 which is provided at the post office

Required Documents:

Passport photo

Original state ID and photocopy

original copy of proof of citizenship and photocopy
