Before starting any application, you should make sure you have an online DMV account. Before choosing to apply online, in person, or by mail, make sure that you meet the requirements for that method.

First time application:

Under 18:

Must be 16 or older to get license

Apply for provisional permit

Must have permit for at least six months

Complete driver's education

Six hours of professional driving instruction

50 hours of practice with a licensed adult 25 or older

10 of the 50 hours must be at night

18 and Over:

Apply for an instruction permit

Once you pass the written exam, you may take driving exam
